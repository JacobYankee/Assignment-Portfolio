Our menu is still growing, but check out some of our customer's favorites!

Larry Burger

A half pound smashburger topped with lettuce, tomato, cheese, and pickles all in a sesame seed bun cooked how you like it. Toppings and bun can be changed upon request. Served with a side of fries.


Club Sandwich

A classic sandwich with ham, turkey, bacon, lettuce, tomatoes, and cheese all between three toasted slices of bread. Fillings and bread can be changed upon request. Served with a side of fries.


Chef Salad

The chef's very own salad recipe. Loaded with hard boiled eggs, cucumbers, tomatoes, turkey, ham, shredded cheddar cheese, tons of leafy greens, and the dressing of your choice. Toppings can be changed upon request. Croutons are available upon request.


Chicken Tenders

A restaurant classic. Four chicken tenders fried for maximum crunch and seasoned for stellar taste. Served with a side of fries and a condiment of your choice.
